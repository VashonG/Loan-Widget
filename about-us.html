About Us
KAPED was founded in 2020 issuing credit cards to small businesses affected by COVID-19. We successfully issued over $1M+ in credit to over 2,000 small businesses, now we aim to make credit available for everyone.
Through KAPED, everyday businesses can embed credit into their platform in a way that is financially and socially rewarding. Borrowers apply online for a line of credit between $500 and $500,000. Businesses and institutions can embed these credit lines into their platform and own the customer journey. KAPED handles all loan servicing on behalf of the matched borrowers and investors.
KAPED is sponsored and backed by Bo Payments.
